# Roadmap

## Search and discovery

- Improve broad market discovery beyond the current all-markets/query/category modes.
- Add a separate long-horizon scan mode for markets closing later than 24 hours when expected edge/profit is unusually high.
- Keep short-term opportunities as the default production monitor, but expose longer-horizon candidates in UI as a separate queue.
- Use manual review labels from `data/review-labels.jsonl` to tune or train the market matcher.
